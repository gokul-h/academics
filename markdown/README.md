### 3. Text Formatting

Bold

```text
I love **bold text**.
I love **bold text**.
Love **is** bold
```

Italic

```text
I love _italic text_.
I love _italic text_.
Love _is_ bold
```

Asteriks and Bold at the same time

```text
I love **_bold and italic text_**.
I love **_bold and italic text_**.
Love **_is_** bold and italic
Love **_is_** bold and italic
```

BlockQuotes

```text
> BlockQuote

> Multipara
> Block Quote

> Nested
>
> > BlockQuotes
```
